About SolvumAi

SolvumAi is a free online math problem solver designed to help students from primary to university level. Our mission is to make learning mathematics easy, accessible, and reliable for all learners.

With SolvumAi, you can type or upload a math problem and receive step-by-step solutions powered by intelligent technology.

This platform was created to support learners and educators who want to better understand mathematics in a convenient and interactive way.
